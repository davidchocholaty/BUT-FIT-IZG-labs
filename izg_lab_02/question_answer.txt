Neefektivni z nasledujicich duvodu:

1. Pouziti operace deleni pro vypocet konstanty k
2. Pouzivani promennych datoveho typu double a jejich zaokrouhlovani pomoci funkce round()

Jedna se o velmi pomale operace a tim padem je zpusobeno, ze algoritmus je neefektivni.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

English version:

Inefficient for the following reasons:

1. Use the division operation to calculate value of constant k
2. Use variables of double data type and their rounding with function round()

These are very slow operations and thus the algorithm is inefficient.
